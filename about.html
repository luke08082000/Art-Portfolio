Caesar Legaspi

As a child, I used to love going to museums and art galleries to explore different kinds of art.
It amazes me how different styles can still be considered as an art form. Even a simple smudge of paint can mean
a thousand words.

Perception is the key to seeing the beauty in art. Being in the moment, understanding the artist's meaning and
expressions. It can tell you a story and it can simply be beautiful in its own way.

I finished college in the University of the Philippines with a Bachelor's Degree in Fine Arts Major in Painting.

Now, aspiring to be a well known artist from the province of Nueva Ecija.

Thank you for visiting the artworks!
