Illustrations Process About the Artist Shop

WAVES OF SUNSET.

one of my favorite moments during a beach vacation, sunsets.

a mix of the colors being reflected every time the sun goes down to the ocean.

the sun, the sky, the water and the waves crashing together to make this piece.

 

MIRROR ON THE WALL.

there are times we find ourselves looking at the mirror,
wondering what's the purpose of our lives.

and sometimes, mirrors break, gets thrown outside and reflects
the light of the sun to make a beautiful pattern despite being
broken.

inspired by broken glass reflections, light bouncing, colors made.

MARBLING UNIVERSE.

different colors mixing together to make a marble like pattern.

like people, different cultures, different ways,
but sometimes get united as one because of a certain purpose or reason.

 

SPECKLED WORLD.

CMYK colors mixed together to create a galaxy like piece.

the world is so big, that this world of ours can be a small speckle inside a galaxy.
that no matter how big the world may seem, there's something bigger.

OCEAN BLUES.

cool blue colors mixed together completing the underwater concept.

inspired by the coral reefs, which gives shelter to other water life forms and provides food and protection.

in honor of world reef awareness day, every June 1st.

 

1 IN 7 BILLION.

we sometimes feel like we're not unique in this world of ours.

but in reality, we are 1 of 1.

inspired by different kinds of people, in one frame.
